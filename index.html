My name is Paul and I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra in beautiful Barcelona.

My research interests lie in financial economics. I've spent time researching how banks cause credit frictions to the real economy, how they interact with regulators and how their CEOs/CFOs words can be used to proxy their uncertainty. Helping me along the way, I've been learning how to apply recent techniques in machine learning and natural language processing to these economic problems. Feel free to look at my research to learn more!
